Our Story

The Beginning

It all started on a serendipitous evening at a local cafe. Hsu, deep in a book, and Aung, trying (and failing) to master latte art, had a chance encounter that sparked a connection neither expected. Their shared love for obscure indie films and late-night talks made it clear: this was more than just a coincidence.

The Journey

Through countless adventures, quiet evenings, and navigating life's ups and downs, their bond only grew stronger. From hiking in the Dolomites to cooking elaborate (and sometimes disastrous) meals, every moment has been a testament to their unwavering commitment and joy in each other's company.
